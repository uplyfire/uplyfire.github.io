About

A small studio with one rule: keep it useful.

Uplyfire is an independent Android studio. We build small apps that solve one problem well — without bloat, dark patterns, or selling your data.

Every app is designed to run mostly on your device. We use established services like Google Play Billing for purchases and Firebase for diagnostics, but never collect more than what's needed to keep things working.

Privacy by default

Your data stays on your device. Analytics and crash reports are consent-gated and pseudonymous.

One-time purchases

No subscriptions for the basic feature set. Pro upgrades unlock once, forever.

Built to last

Apps maintained and updated — not abandoned after launch. Bugs get fixed, fast.

Have feedback or a question?

We answer every email — whether you found a bug, want a new feature, or have a question about your data.
